Destiny

3-D Model made out of foam-core. This is an abstract piece, it is of four staircases that all join at the same point at the top. It is to communicate that which ever path you take in life you will end up where you are meant to be.

Black Birds

Photography. This is a photograph that was taking of two black birds perched on a cross of a church in downtown, Ottawa.

iPhone Case Design

This iPhone case was designed in Adobe Illustrator. Its purpose was to promote the World Wild Life Foundation. The idea to attract buyers was not only the cute panda bear (the icon of the company), but also that a percentage of the cost would go to helping endangered wild life.

Einstein

This piece was designed on Adobe Illustrator. It is the famous Albert Einstein, the style of the art was inspired by Pablo Lobato.

Book Cover Design

Photography and Photoshop. This cover was designed to represent one of the main themes in the novel “The Apprenticeship of Duddy Kravitz”, which was greed.

Godzilla Logo

Paper Cutouts. This logos purpose was to represent the famous Godzilla in the simplest form, only using two colours. The logo represents the image and horror of Godzilla. To symbolize his origin the red circle was placed behind him to symbolize Japan.
